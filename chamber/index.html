News Release

Tahoe ski resorts are still recovering from last years record breaking fire. Sierra at Tahoe saw the worst of the fire. Sierra was not able to open at all last year, but this year, they arguably have benfitted from the fire. Sierra now has 2 more acres of skiiable terrain, and plans to turn those into actual runs or terrain parks are soon to come.
