Demographics

Lake tahoe only has a resident population of around 22,000, but the amount of people in Tahoe is often above 100,000 due to tourism. According to a study conducted in late 2022, tahoe is 80% white, 7% other races, 6% asian, 5% pacific islander, and 2% african american.

Downtown Lake Tahoe

Downtown lake tahoe is a fun place to visit with family and friends. At the base of Heavenly Mountain Resort, one can find shopping, food, and places to rent the newest mountain equipment for activities during any season of the year.

The Lake

The actual lake of lake tahoe is a large reason for the cities amazing tourism. The lake is over 200 feet deep at its deepest spot, and is filled with bright blue water during all 12 months of the year! Lake tahoe has never frozen over.

Winter Sports

Lake tahoe is home to one of the worlds most unique places for winter mountain sports! In Tahoe, you can ski, snowboard, and sled with a view of the lake. People travel from all over to see the beautiful scenery of the lake sourrounded by snow during the winter.

Where States Meet

Lake tahoe is right on the California-Nevada border. Here, one has the unique opportunity to stand in two states at once. Right off of Highway 80 headed towards Reno, take exit 45 to stand one foot in Nevada, and one in California.
