About The Owners

5 Years ago Nate Hollingshaus and his wife, Payton, were living in Carlsbad, and found a lack of organic foods in the area. Payton loved fruit, so Nate set out on his journey to create an all organic fruit stand. This eventually grew into a few food trucks with fruit and drinks. Today, it is one of the largest companies in carlsbad. Nate and Payton are passionate about delicious and healthy food.
