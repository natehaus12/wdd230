Target Audience

General Target Audience

The target audience is all people living in or visiting Lake Tahoe looking for activities being provided in Lake Tahoe. This can include long time residents, new move ins, vacationers, and even small business owners.

Persona Examples

Persona 1: Avid Vacationer

Photo:

Fictional Name: Jared Justice

Job Title: Sales Manager at Apple

Demographics:

  • 55 years old
  • Married
  • Father of four kids
  • Loves to adventure with his family

Goals and Tasks:

Jared has a great work/life balance. He cares about his job, and hopes to soon be the regional sales manager, but he also loves to spend time with his family. They go on a family vacation to a new destinantion every year.

Environment:

Jared is most comfortable in his hotel. He needs wifi to hop on work early in the morning and late at night, but he wants to be out adventuring most of the day. Jared is a thrill seeker, and haves fun doing exhilerating activities. He also enjoys the ambiance of expesive restaurants.

Quote:

"Honey, we're headed to the beautiful Lake Tahoe this summer!!"

User Motivation:

Jared is taking a visit to Tahoe and wants to find some fun things to do with his family, and find great places to eat. The Chamber of commerce site can help him find those things to make for a great vacation.

Persona 2: Small Business Owner

Photo:

Fictional Name: Bob Berrington

Job Title: Future Owner and CEO of Berrington Kayak Rentals

Demographics:

  • 30 years old
  • Single
  • No kids
  • Loves all nature related activities

Goals and Tasks:

Bob has always wanted to own his own business. He developed his love for Tahoe's nature from a young age, and wants to help people see it for themselves through his new business. He wants to rent out kayaks right on the lake, but he is concerned about the amount of competition.

Environment:

Jared loves being outdoors and in nature. He would live in the woods if it was plausible. He has lived at home since he was a boy, and wants to move in to his own place. He thinks this small business could be his way to his dream home.

Quote:

"Tahoe is the prettiest place on Earth!"

User Motivation:

Bob wants to start a Kayak rental business right on the Lake, but he is a little concerned about the amount of competition. On the Chamber of Commerce site he can find these businesses and see how competitive this space would be.
