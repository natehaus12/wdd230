Typography

I will be using various different fonts. The three I have picked out right now are Roboto, Raleway, and Montserrat. I may add more later, but I think these three are great. Robot is for all my main, smaller text. Raleway is for all my large headers. Montserrat is for all my sub headers. You can see them exemplified on this page. My header and footer are Raleway, my nav bar is montserrat. and the main section is all Roboto.
